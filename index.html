So Happy and Honored

So Happy and Honored,11. May 2021

was very happy to meet the Dragon Boat team who will be representing China at the “Tokyo Olympics Dragon Boat Performance Event” today, and I’m also extremely honoured to be the “Dragon Boat Ambassador” and the “Olympic Dragon Boat Performance Event Ambassador”. I hope to physically support the Dragon Boat event so it can become an official competitive Olympic event. In China, “dragon” is traditionally considered a cultural symbol and an important image, and dragon boating is also a traditional sporting event originating from China. In recent years, many more countries around the world have quickly gained interest in the sport, and it was officially made a competitive event at the 2010 Asia World Games. I hope the Olympic Games will also make it an official competitive event soon as well so it can shine at the world’s most prestigious sporting stage!
